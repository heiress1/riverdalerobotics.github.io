Apol - Le

Apol-Le named after the Apollo missions and our lead mentor, Nhat Le, is R3P2's 4th robot. Using experience gained from 2018 FIRST Power Up on how to build effective elevators, Apol-Le uses an elevator to move our first ever pivoting arm up and down. Our 2019 robot uses a screwing mechanism to push itself up onto the third level of the habitat.
