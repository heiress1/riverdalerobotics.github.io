2023 - Centerstage

Tech Rascals

The Tech Rascals made use of a mechanum drive robot, with a single claw design. The arm of the robot could rotate 360 degrees, allowing it to place game pieces on the board from both the front, and the back. With the help of tensorflow, the bot used vision to detect team props in the autonomous period, and place game pieces down for extra points.

Season Achievements:

  • Central Scarbrough Semi-Finalist
  • 2nd Place Connect Award Central Scarbrough
  • 2nd Place Control Award Bolton
  • Judge's Choice Award Bolton

Tech Racers

The Tech Racers made a fully 3D printed robot! With a black-pink colour scheme, this robot made use of its light weight to zip around the field. Using tank drive, the robot could push the game pieces into the scoring zone for points. It made use of tensorflow to detect team props in the autonomous period, and push game pieces to the correct area.

Season Achievements:

  • Central Scarbrough Design Award

Tech Runners

Made by the Riverdale 12th grade robotics class, this bot made use of mechanum drive to strafe around the field. It had a 2 claw intake, allowing it to pick up 2 game pieces at a time and score them on the board. Making use of tensorflow, this bot could detect team props in the autonomous period, and place a piece in the marked area. It could then score another note on the game board for an incredible 45 point auto!

Season Achievements:

  • Central Scarbrough 3rd Place Design Award
  • Bolton Winning Alliance
  • Bolton Control Award
  • Provincials Semi-Finalists
