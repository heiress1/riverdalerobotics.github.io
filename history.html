The impact of FIRST is everlasting on our students

From in highschool to the workplace, the skills, experiences and memories that students have achieved over the years in R3P2 last with them forever. FIRST has a strong influence on the minds of tomorrow, because of this over 90% of the grade 12 students leaving our team each pursue a STEM program in post-secondary.
